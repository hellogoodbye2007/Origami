Interesting facts about swans:

  • They mate for life, like humans.
  • They are very territorial during mating season, so don't approach them then!
  • They live for 20-30 years.

Interesting facts about foxes:

  • Foxes make 40 different sounds!
  • They have a really good sense of hearing.
  • A group of foxes is called a skulk.

Interesting facrs about frogs:

  • A group of frogs is called an army.
  • They can absorb water through their skin so they don't have to drink.
  • The South African sharp-nosed frog is three inches long but can jump up to 130 inches!
